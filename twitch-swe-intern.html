Twitch - Software Engineering Intern

  • May 2022 - August 2022

During the summer of 2022, I worked at Twitch on the Web Video Clients team as a Software Engineering Intern. My main project was to certify Twitch's video player for the new PS5 application. This included testing and debugging TypeScript and native C++ code, as well as writing lots of documentation. I solved many bugs including fixing an end-of-stream state change error occuring on many platforms by implementing a video playback buffer fix. I enabled local and production development on the PS5 Dev Kits by developing controller support and solving networking bugs. I performed performance tests for video playback using metrics such as latency, buffer size, dropped frames, and quality watched to verify the video playback Web Worker design on the PS5. My role involved cross team collaboration with engineers to solve many problems in a timely fashion.
