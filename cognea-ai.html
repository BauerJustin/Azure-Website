Cognea AI

  • December 2022 - August 2023
  • Technologies: React, NodeJS, OpenAI GPT & Whisper APIs, Azure

I worked on Cognea AI, which provided users with a practice interview platform powered by the latest Generative AI models.

I led the ideation process hypothesizing a need for an improved interview practice tool and developed a Minimum Viable Product (“MVP”) to test said hypothesis, providing users an AI practice interviewer that conducts verbal mock interviews with custom interview questions and follow ups, as well as personalized feedback.

The MVP web application and back-end services including the AI interview flow, prompts conducting the interview and providing feedback, AI interview text to speech, user speech to text voice processing, interview voice and speech animations, user authentication/management, data storage/logging, and deployment, were developed by myself using React and Azure Active Directory, Static Web Apps, and Cognitive Services in the front end and Azure Functions, Cosmos DB, NodeJS, and Open AI GPT & Whisper APIs in the back end.

In order to build an online presence, I created and deployed HTML landing page, setting up domain and hosting on Azure, to acquire waitlist users prior to product launch, added blogs and content to improve SEO ranking.

After developing the MVP, I executed the product launch by emailing our waitlist and posting video ads on social media, which resulted in over 50 sign ups.
